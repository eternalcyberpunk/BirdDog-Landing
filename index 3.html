What you get

The intelligence your competitors don't have.

Every address comes with property history, permit data, aging system flags, and an AI-generated pitch tuned to what actually works in the trades.

Full property profile

Year built, sqft, beds, baths, stories, foundation type, owner name, ownership duration, estimated equity — pulled the second an address is entered.

Complete permit history

Every install, every replacement, every addition, every upgrade. See exactly what's been done — and what hasn't — going back 25 years.

System age inference

If a replacement permit exists, we use it. If not, we calculate probable age from build year. Either way, your tech knows before they knock.

Right-sized recommendations

Manual J-lite tonnage calculations based on square footage and climate. Flags undersized homes that need an upgrade, not just a swap.

AI-generated pitch

A personalized opener for every house — tuned to the specific flags we found. "Your home expanded in 2015, did the HVAC ever get resized?"

Auto-priced proposal tiers

Good, better, best pricing calibrated to the home's size, system type, and regional labor costs. Close rates spike 2–3x with on-site estimates.

Territory heatmap

See every aging system in your service area on a map. Auto-generated farming lists by zip. Route optimization for door-knock campaigns.

Customer cross-reference

Import your existing customer list — we'll flag every past client whose system is reaching end-of-life so you call them before they call a competitor.

Trade-specific modules

HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, solar. Same app, different intelligence layer. One subscription per trade you run.
